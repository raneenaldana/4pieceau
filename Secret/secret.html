About

Project 4piece was founded by Nice Arneb Thunder, CEO of Thunderbolt Entertainment, aimed towards creating a better world for performers under a global market through joining multiple singing venues, namely Ensemble Square to the Thunderbolt Entertainment company.

Aims

  • Expand the entertainment industry to global markets
  • Merge Ensemble Square with Thunderbolt Entertainment
  • Popularize music culture to wider ranges of demographics (e.g. children, elderly, etc.)
